I'm Evana Ann Benny,
Creative Technologist & Storyteller.

Hi, I’m Evana — a curious creative with a passion for storytelling, technology, and the wonders of the universe. With a background in Computer Science and a strong interest in agritech innovation, I enjoy exploring how AI, IoT, and data can solve real-world problems. Outside of work, I’m an avid writer with a love for essays, stories, and film reviews, some of which have even been recognized and published. I’m also deeply into sports — whether it's playing badminton and football or watching cricket, football, and MMA. I find joy in photography, character psychology, and reading about astrophysics. I thrive in collaborative spaces, love meaningful conversations, and enjoy bringing ideas to life through teamwork and creativity.
